Whitney's Meal Plan
With the Flow
4-Week Grocery Lists
Dairy-Free Gluten-Free
Weeks 1–3: Standard Phase (2 meals + 1 snack per day)
Week 4 (Days 22–30): Full Luteal Phase (3 meals + 1 snack per day)
Most pantry staples (spices, oils, sauces) only need to be purchased once. Weeks 2–4 call them out as "check if any left" so you only restock what you actually need.
